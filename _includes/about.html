What is zmNinja?

zmNinja is an awesome home security app that works along with ZoneMinder

Designed with love

zmNinja has a beautifully fluid UI, designed with love. It's a big improvement over using the ZoneMinder console on your phone. Try it and I hope you'll agree

Time Saving

zmNinja is intuitive. No heavy manuals to read. Enter your ZM details (yeah, the cgi-bin needs to be watched out for) and get going!

Multi-OS

zmNinja works on Android as well as iOS. Well, don't just stop there. It also works on Linux, Windows and Mac desktops. And by 'works' I mean 'works gorgeously'. No second hand love

Mobile-first

Okay, that was a bit of lie above about no second-hand love. zmNinja is a mobile first app - but I'm serious - it's also gorgeous on Desktops

Source for you

Don't like something? Hack it and contribute back. Nothing like having source, eh?

Can't live without ZoneMinder

zmNinja without Zoneminder is not like bread without butter. It's more like trying to breath without air. zmNinja needs ZoneMinder to work
